01 — WHO WE ARE

Two DJS.
ONE NAME.

Bermuda is a tech house trio based in Denver, Colorado. Named for the feeling of disappearing — the moment you stop checking your phone, stop watching the clock, and surrender entirely to the music.

Our sets live at the intersection of groove and euphoria: driving enough to keep you moving, warm enough to make you feel something.

We play clubs, festivals, and private events across Colorado and beyond. You went in for a quick drink. You left at 2am. You're welcome.
